ABOUT US



Streetwise Catering is a fake website designed by Jake Satow in a intro to CS class. I thought of the name Streetwise Catering by doing a random word generator and finding words that sounded fancy. I came up with my menu items from mostly a youtuber known as Joshua Weissman, a chef on youtube. All the following images are from Joshua's page, and you can go feel free to look at all the videos linked to all the images.


I first started off by building the homepage. My original navbar was actually made with list tags, but I did several hours of research to figure out how to make a dropdown navbar with div tags. I stepped it up one more noch when I realized that I could make a side navbar, but also with dropdowns. I thought that was pretty cool. I stuck that one out. I added a title to the homepage, and then I started working. I made a specials menu, and I built the contact page. Now how could I get that darn submit button to work? I met with my schools tech team, and I figured out how to use phpserver, and I made a php file that would allow data to be emailted to my phone, simply from filling out a form. Now that all the hard part is done, I had to find some food for the menus. I went to youtubes cooking side for the first time, and I cam accross Joshua Weissman. He had some pretty dope food on his channel, so I decided to use some of his food on my menu. Now I'm here building an about page and telling my story. Thank you for visiting my website!
